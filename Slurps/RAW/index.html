Our Story

A little boy born with a sweet tooth, grew up to be a foodie. On the journey to satisfy his ineluctable love for shakes, he realised that he could never have enough of it.So he decided to blend his own.

At the first sip, he knew it was magic and wanted to share it with the world.
And then Slurps was born, in the city called Coimbatore.

About us

When it comes to desserts, taste and texture are the indispensable partners that make the delicacy palatable.This has been our Food Mantra! At Slurps, we meticulously craft our desserts and blend our shakes with precision to bestow your taste buds with an elite experience.

Launched in mid- 2022 as a cloud kitchen,we kick-started with milkshakes and thickshakes.Overwhelmed with the response and after six months of R&D,we opened our first outlet by the end of 2022.This time, with a scintillating list of desserts- thickshakes, milkshakes, smoothies,mojitos,sundaes,waffles,bobas and a lot more in the pipeline.Our outlet has been themed to bring out the youth in you - “Orange, grey and white to make feel delight”. Come and experience the burst of energy that we are!
